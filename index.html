How to Play

XO Arena brings the classic Tic-Tac-Toe game to your Discord server. It's simple: get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to win. Games will time out after 60 seconds of inactivity.

1

Start a game with `/play` (vs. a friend) or `/playbot` (vs. the bot).

2

Take turns placing your mark by clicking the buttons on the grid.

3

The first to get three in a row wins. If the grid is full, it's a draw!

All Commands

/play @opponent

Starts a game with another player.

/playbot

Starts a game against the AI bot.

/stop

Stops the current game.

/leaderboard

Shows the server's top players.

/stats [user]

View your or another player's stats.

/setlanguage [th/en/jp]

Changes the bot's language.

/settimezone [timezone]

Sets your personal timezone.

/feedback [message]

Send feedback directly to the developers.
